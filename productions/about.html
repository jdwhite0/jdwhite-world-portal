About

Built on production. Operating as architecture.

JD Productions is an architecture venture company — designing identity infrastructure, AI systems, and digital worlds on a foundation built through a decade of production at the highest level.

Founder

JD White

CEO, Director, Producer — Principal Owner & Operator of JD Productions Inc. Started in 2014 while at Jacksonville State University. Incorporated April 2018 in Tampa, FL. Origin: Dothan, Alabama. Raised in the Atlanta metro area. Education: Jacksonville State University — Applied Manufacturing Engineering & Management. Kappa Alpha Psi, Inc.

At JSU, JD White created the first Snapchat Geofilters deployed on campus — filters still used by thousands of students. He served as video producer for the university's Marketing & Communications department and was appointed photographer for JSU President Dr. John M. Beehler during the 2016 football season.

JD Productions was incorporated in April 2018 — structured from the start as a multi-division enterprise. In 2026, the company evolved beyond production-as-identity into a systems architecture company: ACCESS, JYSON, Founder OS, and the JD AI Operating System now form the technology layer behind every world JD Productions builds.
